title: App Flow
---

- Open the storefront ui.

![Storefront ui](../../images/storefront_ui.png)

- Click on `BROWSE ITEM CATALOG`

![Storefront catalog](../../images/storefront_catalog.png)

- Click on any of the product from the catalog.

![Storefront product info](../../images/storefront_prod_info.png)

- Login using your credentials.

![Storefront login](../../images/storefront_login.png)

- Home screen after login looks like below.

![Storefront login catalog](../../images/storefront_login_catalog.png)

- Place the orders.

![Storefront order](../../images/storefront_user_order.png)

- After successfully placing the order, you will see something like below.

![Storefront order confirmed](../../images/storefront_user_order_confirmed.png)

- User information will be displayed in the profile section.

![Storefront user profile](../../images/storefront_user_profile.png)

- Finally, logout as below.

![Storefront logout](../../images/storefront_logout.png)
